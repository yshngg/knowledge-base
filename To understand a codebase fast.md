To understand a codebase fast,  
It's all about context and the past.  
First, know why it exists,  
What problem it's trying to fix.  
Then, learn how it's solving this task,  
Not the details, just what makes it last.  
Next, see the big components in play,  
How they work together, in a way.  
Then, pick one part, start to explore,  
Understand it well, and then some more.  
If you get stuck, don't despair,  
Just move on, and leave it there.  
With time and effort, you'll see,  
The codebase will become clear to thee.  
Math and coding are quite the same,  
A way to express abstract things in a concrete frame.  
Follow these steps, and you'll soon be  
Contributing to the codebase, happily.
